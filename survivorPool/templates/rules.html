League Rules

Core rules

  • $50 buy-in per person into the pot.
  • Each NFL team may be used only once per player per season.
  • Each loss adds $10 to the pot.
  • Loss while picking the week's biggest favorite (or co-favorite) adds $25.
  • Unlimited half-loss credits when you correctly pick an underdog of 5+ points.
  • Sunday 1:05 PM ET: picks lock; no pick in the app = auto LOSS (posted in League Chat).
  • Thursday Night: if you pick a Thursday team, post it in League Chat before kickoff.
  • Everyone finishes the season - no quitting to avoid payments.

Keep playing after a loss

This is not one-and-done survivor. You keep picking every week; losses fund the pot.

Tie-breaker (if needed)

  1. Predict all Week 18 game winners.
  2. Predict total points in the final game of the season.
  3. Best Week 18 record, or closest to the final game total, wins the tie-break.

Prize money

After 3rd place is paid from the pot:

  • 3rd: buy-in back ($50); tie for 3rd splits one $50.
  • 2nd: 25% of remaining pot.
  • 1st: 75% of remaining pot.
